Checkers

This was my first project at General Assembly bootcamp. I used this opportunity to really dive into vanilla Javascript.

Choosing the UI styling, I was inspired by 2001: A Space Odyssey. You know, that obscure scene with Dave in a Renascence-styled room...

In case you haven't ever played, checkers is a strategy board game for two players which involve diagonal moves of uniform game pieces and captures by jumping over opponent pieces.
